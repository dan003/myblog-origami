Here a some steps creating kusudamas.

A kusudama, simply put is an origami ball made from many sheets of papers. You will find these instructions helpful.

  1. Obtain a paper size similar to the diagrams recommended size.
  2. Choose color that matches the picture of the kusudama.
  3. Assemble with or without glue.

Step three is the most difficult part after you have folded x number of units. This can be frustrating because paper is often slippery or brittle. At night is always a good time to assemble your units. No one will bother you then, and we are generally more relaxed at the end of the day.
